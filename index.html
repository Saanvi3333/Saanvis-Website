About Me

Hi, my name is Saanvi and I am a third-year Computer Science student specializing in Cognitive Science at Queen’s University. I first got into Computer Science when I took an Intro to Coding course in high school, and I immediately fell in love with coding and creating projects. Since then, I have been fascinated by how technology works and how it can be used to solve real-world problems. Outside of my studies, I enjoy a good coffee break, especially from Starbucks, getting lost in a good book, or finding a TV show to watch. I also love connecting with new people, whether it is to make friendships or network with industry professionals. I always bring a positive attitude and my bubbly personality to everything I do. As I continue my studies, I hope to gain more experience in building meaningful projects, learning new programming languages, and exploring the intersection of technology and human behavior. I am excited to take on challenges that allow me to be creative, think critically, and grow both personally and professionally.
